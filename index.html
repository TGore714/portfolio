My Story

Hello, my name is Tyler Gore, and my journey as a proficient independent software developer has been nothing short of exhilarating. Although I am also an accomplished EMT, I want to share my story with a strong focus on my passion for software development. Right after graduating from high school, I took a bold step and pursued EMT training at VoTech. While studying to become an EMT, I also held a position as a gymnastics coach, helping young athletes excel in their sport. After completing EMT school and securing a position as an EMT, I spent two fulfilling years in this role. But then, my life took a significant turn when I met my significant other. Within a year, we tied the knot, and together, we decided to relocate to Texas, embarking on a new chapter in our lives. It was during this time in Texas that I stumbled upon my true passion – software development. I had always been fascinated by technology, but it was here that I decided to explore it further. Under the guidance of seasoned software engineers, I commenced my online learning journey, eager to soak up every bit of knowledge. While in the process of obtaining my Texas state EMS license, I seized an opportunity and started my career at Amazon as a driver. It was a valuable experience that taught me a lot about efficiency and logistics. Finally, the moment I had been waiting for arrived, and I successfully received my EMS license. I rejoined the EMS field but couldn't ignore the magnetic pull of software development. I started collaborating on software projects for my personal clientele, embracing the challenge and joy of bringing their ideas to life. Today, I proudly balance both worlds – software development and the EMS industry. I manage multiple websites of my own, all built from the ground up, and continue to build software solutions for various clients. It's a journey that has taught me the importance of dedication, continuous learning, and finding fulfillment in the work I do. I am excited about what the future holds and the endless possibilities that lie ahead of this exciting path of software development.
